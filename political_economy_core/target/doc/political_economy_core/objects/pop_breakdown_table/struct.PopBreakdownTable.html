Pop Breakdown Table

Stores the information and ‘rows’ of a population’s data table. Each

Fields

table: Vec<PBRow>

The table of all our data, broken up by the row’s categories. Species(Cohort, Subtype), Culture(Class, Generation), Ideology(Wave, Faction)

total: usize

The sum of all the people in the table. Keep updated with any change.
